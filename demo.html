Envisioning a Generative Canvas

Please enjoy the two demos that we've prepared.

Demo #1 envisions our final product - we are currently in the process of building Osmosis's web interface, but all ads featured in this demo were developed by Osmosis software with no post-processing.

We made Demo #2 after meeting with Jess Lee to express our thanks with a custom champagne ad with her name on it.

Demo #2 offers an inside look into our proprietary Ad Generation technology, where we first generate a Champagne Advertisement Dall-E and then blend real products into the synthetic content using Osmosis's image harmonization technology. It's important to note that we expect DALL-E will eventually have a public API, but there are other LLMs we can rely on for image generation as well (another transformer model, Stable Diffusion, just opensourced its weights as an example.)

We are posting these demos to share our vision and to demonstrate the functionality of Osmosis' core technology.
